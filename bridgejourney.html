Discover the World's Most Iconic Bridges

Explore the beauty and engineering marvels of bridges from around the globe.

Golden Gate Bridge

An iconic suspension bridge connecting San Francisco Bay and the Pacific Ocean.

Brooklyn Bridge

A historic bridge in New York City, connecting Manhattan and Brooklyn.

Tower Bridge

A combined bascule and suspension bridge in London, over the River Thames.

Sydney Harbour Bridge

A steel through arch bridge across Sydney Harbour, Australia.

Ponte Vecchio

A medieval stone closed-spandrel segmental arch bridge over the Arno River in Florence, Italy.

Millau Viaduct

A cable-stayed bridge that spans the valley of the River Tarn near Millau in southern France.
