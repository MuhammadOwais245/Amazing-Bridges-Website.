Guided Tours

Tour 1: Bridge History and Architecture

Description: Explore the history and architectural marvels of iconic bridges like the Millau Viaduct and Pont du Gard. Learn about their construction, engineering feats, and historical significance. This tour is ideal for history enthusiasts and architecture buffs.

Schedule: Available daily, starting at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

Duration: Approximately 2 hours.

Guide: Our guides are experts in bridge engineering and local history, ensuring an informative and engaging experience.

Price: $50 per person.

Tour 2: Nighttime Bridge Photography

Description: Capture stunning nighttime views of illuminated bridges and learn photography tips from professional photographers. Equipment rental is included, allowing participants to capture memorable shots of these architectural wonders under the stars.

Schedule: Offered on weekends, starting at 8:00 PM.

Duration: Approximately 3 hours.

Guide: Our photography guides are seasoned professionals with experience in nighttime photography techniques and settings.

Price: $75 per person (includes photography equipment rental).

Tour 3: Bridge Maintenance and Technology

Description: Gain insight into the maintenance and technological innovations behind modern bridges. Visit maintenance facilities and learn about ongoing projects that ensure the safety and longevity of these structures.

Schedule: Available weekdays upon request.

Duration: Approximately 4 hours.

Guide: Led by engineers and maintenance experts who oversee the upkeep and technological advancements in bridge construction.

Price: $65 per person.

Tour 4: River Cruise and Bridge History

Description: Embark on a scenic river cruise that highlights bridges from a unique perspective. Learn about the cultural and historical significance of bridges while enjoying picturesque views along the riverbanks.

Schedule: Offered twice a week, starting at 11:00 AM.

Duration: Approximately 3 hours.

Guide: Local historians and river cruise experts provide insights into bridge architecture and local heritage.

Price: $60 per person.

Tour 5: Bridge Food and Culinary Tour

Description: Explore bridges while sampling local cuisine and culinary delights. Visit nearby cafes, markets, and restaurants known for their bridge-themed dishes and specialties.

Schedule: Offered on select weekdays, starting at 12:00 PM.

Duration: Approximately 3 hours.

Guide: Food experts and local guides share insights into the cultural and culinary significance of bridges in the region.

Price: $55 per person (includes food tastings).

Tour 6: Family Fun Bridge Adventure

Description: A family-friendly tour with interactive activities and games centered around bridges. Engage in educational games, scavenger hunts, and storytelling sessions suitable for all ages.

Schedule: Offered on weekends, starting at 10:00 AM.

Duration: Approximately 2.5 hours.

Guide: Experienced guides trained in educational programs for children and families.

Price: $40 per adult, $25 per child (under 12 years old).
